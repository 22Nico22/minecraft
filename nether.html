Nether

The Nether is a dangerous dimension filled with fire, lava, and unique mobs. It is often used as a shortcut for fast travel and is a source of exclusive materials.

Creation

The Nether is only created when a player builds a Nether portal and enters it for the first time. Unlike the Overworld, it does not exist until accessed.

Environment

The Nether has no day-night cycle. The environment is always dark and dangerous. Lava is common, and the only natural light source is glowing blocks such as Glowstone. Many unique mobs spawn only in the Nether, including Ghasts, Piglins, and Blazes.

Naturally created Blocks

Naturally created blocks in the Nether are generated during world creation and include unique materials and terrain features.

Seeds

Seeds affect Nether generation as well. Using the same seed ensures the Nether terrain, structures, and biome distribution remain consistent across worlds.
